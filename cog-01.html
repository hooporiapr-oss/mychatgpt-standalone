The React

A single-beat basketball reaction drill. A cell lights up. You react. The next cue comes fast. This trains visual attention, timing, pressure response, and decision speed.

Basketball Money connection: money opportunities move fast. The player who reacts late often loses the opening.
What it tests

Reaction, timing, focus, visual cue recognition, and fast decision execution.

How to play

Tap the purple cell as fast as possible. Perfect taps score more. Wrong taps or missed cues cost lives.
