The Recall

A basketball memory drill. Numbered cells light up. You memorize the positions. Then you tap them back in order. This trains sequence memory, possession-long focus, and recall under pressure.

Basketball Money connection: reaction gets you started. Recall helps you remember the assignment, the opportunity, the sequence, and the next move.
What it tests

Memory, order, sequence recall, focus, patience, and execution after the cue disappears.

How to play

Watch the numbered cells. When they disappear, tap them back in order: 1, 2, 3, 4, 5.
