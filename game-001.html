Weak Answers
  • I am him.
  • I am unstoppable.
  • I am different.
  • I work hard.
Stronger Answers
  • I can guard full court without fouling.
  • I create open shots when help defense steps up.
  • I stay steady when my role changes.
  • I make the simple play under pressure.

3Q
Pressure The game changes. Value gets exposed.
+
Game Situation

Score is close. Crowd is loud. Coach is watching. Your defender is pressuring you. Your team needs one reliable player.

Why should that reliable player be you?

Answer With Behavior
  • I will make the right read when help comes.
  • I will defend without reaching.
  • I will communicate the coverage.
  • I will take care of the ball.
  • I will not let missed shots change my effort.
When my first option gets taken away, I will __________.
4Q
Decision What are you going to prove this week?
+
Decision Point

A player who understands value does not just say, “I want more opportunity.”

They ask: What do I need to prove so opportunity makes sense?

AMore reliable effort
BOne specific skill
CCoach trust
DPressure decisions
ECommunication
FBody language
Weak Actions
  • Work harder
  • Be better
  • Stay focused
  • Lock in
Strong Actions
  • Take 100 game-speed shots after practice three times this week.
  • Watch 15 minutes of film and write down three bad decisions.
  • Talk on every defensive possession in practice tomorrow.
  • Ask coach one thing I need to become more trusted in.
This week I will prove __________ by doing __________.
