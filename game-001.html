Foundation

The first lesson is simple: the game on the court creates value off the court.

A player is not only playing for stats. A player is creating attention, trust, proof, relationships, content, reputation, and future access.

Value creation

Basketball money starts when skill, attention, trust, and opportunity connect.

The business side of basketball is not only contracts. It includes training, camps, branding, content, sponsorship, coaching, scouting, community, and relationships.

Skill creates proof.

People believe what they can see repeated.

Attention creates reach.

Reach helps the right people discover the player.

Trust creates opportunity.

People open doors for players they trust.

Mindset warning

If you only see basketball as points, you may miss the business around the points.

Playbook warning: a player can be talented and still miss opportunities because they never learned how the basketball economy works.

Player value

In the basketball economy, the player is not only a participant. The player can become an asset.

An asset creates value. A player creates value through performance, leadership, attention, knowledge, community, and influence.

Action mindset
