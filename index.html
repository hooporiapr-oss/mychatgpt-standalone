What This Is

Hoops Money is not hype. It is a basketball financial literacy program built around value, proof, and decisions.

01

Value

Before money shows up, value has to be real. Players learn why someone would choose, trust, recruit, sponsor, or invest in them.

02

Habits

The program exposes whether a player's daily actions match what they say they want. No excuses. No fake answers.

03

Money Decisions

Players learn that basketball can open doors, but money, opportunity, brand, and reputation require discipline.
