Cognitive Reaction Hoops

The game is moving fast. The best players do not just know what to do. They process, remember, see, sequence, and adjust faster.

Why This Belongs Here

Hoops Money teaches value and decisions. Cognitive Reaction Hoops trains the player’s decision system: eyes, timing, reaction, memory, pressure, and adjustment.

01
The React Reaction and rhythm. Trains the player to process a visual cue and respond before the next beat.
Basketball use: closeouts, loose balls, quick reads
02
The Recall Working memory. Trains the player to hold multiple positions or instructions while still executing.
Basketball use: plays, defensive assignments, spacing
03
The Reflex Visual processing. Trains the player to scan the court quickly and identify what matters first.
Basketball use: court vision, help defense, passing windows
04
The Replay Sequence memory. Trains the player to remember order, timing, and movement under pressure.
Basketball use: sets, reads, rotations, late-game execution
05
The Ritmo Change detection. Trains the player to notice what moved, what opened, and what changed in the possession.
Basketball use: defensive rotations, broken plays, advantage reads
Every drill is a basketball read. Every read is a decision. Every decision reveals readiness.
