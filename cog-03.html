The Reflex

A multi-target basketball reflex drill. Several cells flash at once. You remember which cells lit up, then find all targets by tapping them in any order.

Basketball Money connection: COG 02 trained ordered recall. COG 03 trains reflex recall — seeing multiple signals, remembering them, and finding the right targets fast.
What it tests

Multi-target memory, reflex recognition, visual scanning, fast target finding, accuracy, and pressure recovery.

How to play

Watch the flash. Remember every target. When they disappear, tap all correct cells in any order.
